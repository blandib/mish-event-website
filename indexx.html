Our Story

From Balloon Artist to Full Decor Specialist

Celebrations Designed to Shine began as a passion project in 2015 when our founder, Jessica, started creating balloon arrangements for friends' birthday parties. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a thriving business as word spread about our unique designs and attention to detail.

Today, we're a team of 5 dedicated decor specialists serving Johannesburg and surrounding areas. We've expanded beyond balloons to offer full event styling services, but we've never lost our commitment to the creativity and joy that started it all.

We believe that every celebration—no matter how big or small—deserves to be beautiful, memorable, and uniquely tailored to the people being celebrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for standard events, and 8-12 weeks for weddings and large events, especially during peak season (October-December).

Do you offer delivery and setup?

Yes, we offer delivery and professional setup for all our services. Delivery fees are based on location. For hired items, we offer both delivery and pickup options.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due 7 days before your event. For hired items, we also require a refundable damage deposit.
