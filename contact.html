Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for standard events, and 8-12 weeks for weddings and large events, especially during peak season (October-December).

Do you offer delivery and setup?

Yes, we offer delivery and professional setup for all our services. Delivery fees are based on location. For hired items, we offer both delivery and pickup options.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due 7 days before your event. For hired items, we also require a refundable damage deposit.
