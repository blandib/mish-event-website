Can I make changes after booking?

Yes, minor changes can be made up to 14 days before your event. For major adjustments, please contact us as early as possible so we can accommodate your request.

What happens if I need to cancel?

Cancellations made more than 30 days in advance are eligible for a partial refund. Cancellations within 30 days may forfeit the deposit. Please refer to our cancellation policy for full details.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! We tailor packages to suit your event size, style, and budget. Reach out with your vision and we'll help bring it to life.

Are your items sanitized between uses?

Yes, all items are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized after each use to ensure safety and hygiene for every client.

Can you help with last-minute bookings?

We do our best to accommodate urgent requests depending on availability. For last-minute bookings, please contact us directly to check if we can assist.

Do you offer consultations or site visits?

Yes, we offer consultations to help plan your event and ensure everything aligns with your vision. Site visits are available for larger setups or custom designs.
